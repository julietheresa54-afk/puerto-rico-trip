Arrival Day — Welcome to Puerto Rico
Land at SJU at 8:38 PM. Stop in Piñones on the way into San Juan for a taste of local Puerto Rican food — fresh coconut water, alcapurrias, and empanadillas from the roadside kiosks. Check into La Concha Resort in Condado and settle in.

Old San Juan — History, Art & Shopping

Explore & Discover

Free Day Suggestions

Tuesday · July 28
Old San Juan
🏰
Castillo San Felipe del Morro
Iconic 16th-century fort perched on a cliff above the Atlantic. Sweeping ocean views and incredible for photos. Allow 1.5–2 hours.
🎨
Calle del Cristo & Boutique Shopping
The main shopping street of Old San Juan — local jewelry, handmade crafts, Puerto Rican rum shops, and colorful fashion boutiques.
🍽️
Lunch at La Factoria or Marmalade
La Factoria is a legendary bar/restaurant with hidden rooms. Marmalade is a romantic fine dining spot with great cocktails.
🌸
Paseo de la Princesa
A beautiful promenade along the city walls with fountains, local artisans, and stunning views of San Juan Bay.
Wednesday · July 29
Santurce & Condado Evening
🖼️
Santurce Street Art Walk
Santurce is home to some of the Caribbean's most vibrant murals. Self-guided walk around Calle Loíza and Calle Canals — every wall is a masterpiece.
🛍️
Calle Loíza Shopping
The hippest street in San Juan — indie boutiques, vintage shops, local designers, and great coffee spots.
🌅
Sunset at Condado Beach
The Condado beach strip glows beautifully at sunset. Grab cocktails at one of the beachfront bars and watch the sky turn gold.
🍸
Condado Nightlife Strip
Condado has a vibrant restaurant and nightlife scene. Try Avocado, Tijuana's Bar & Grill, or SoFo for a lively evening out.
